What is THEC Talks ?

THEC Talks is an opportunity for students to exchange experiences and insights about web-related technical subjects. The first edition took place at HEC Paris on May 23rd 2011, and was a part of the Digital Business for Innovation certificate.

This event was organized by and for the students of HEC Paris. The talks all have one rule in common : practice over theory. That is why efforts were made to be as much hands-on as possible, with lots of live demos and visuals.

We made everything available on this website : videos, slides and useful links. Feel free to have a look at them !
